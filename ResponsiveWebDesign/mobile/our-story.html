Our Name

We didn't forget the spell check...the unique spelling of EDJE represents the 3 original founders: Erica Gingrich, Dave Michels and Joelle Rubcich Enterprises. With over 49 years of combined IT experience in leading edge technologies, we thought it should be reflected in our name.

Building custom apps is our sweet spot. We focus on building a company where the technically driven can excel. Our excellence translates into high quality solutions for our clients. We have a ton of fun and take a lot of pride in being the best "true geeks" by continually educating ourselves on the latest technologies.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is to utilize our passion for technology to help our clients achieve their business and technology goals. We founded Leading EDJE under the principal of "under selling and over delivering". Our clients consistently provide feedback that they appreciate this approach and our talent sells itself.

Our Goal

Professionals spend a very large part of their lives at work. We truly believe it can and needs to be enjoyable. Our goal when founding Leading EDJE was to make work fun. We are not interested in setting any records by being the biggest; rather we want to enjoy being the best!
